Bookay - Buy and Sell Books Marketplace

Bookay

UI/UX Designer, Front-End Developer

Bookay is a community marketplace like carousell but for book lovers. Client wants a simple webapp that buys and sells second-hand books or brand new books. It also has an option to trade. Bookay doesn't medle in terms of payment. Buyer and seller will communicate with each other to sell products. However, future launch of the website will include in-app payment.

Tools used for UI: SketchApp for wireframing, Invision for Rapid Prototyping

Front-End: HTML5,CSS3, Bootstrap, Javascript

You can read more about my design process and user journey building Bookay on my case studies.
